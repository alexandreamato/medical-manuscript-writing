# Abstract Examples Index (Medical Research)

All abstract example cites point to the local files below. Each template includes a worked medical example.

1. Version 1 (Challenge → Contribution) — typical RCT abstract.
   `Version 1: Introduce the clinical / methodological gap, then in one to two sentences present the study contribution that addresses it.`
   `references/examples/abstract/template-a.md`

2. Version 2 (Challenge → Insight → Contribution) — observational study or target-trial-emulation abstract.
   `Version 2: Introduce the clinical gap, then one sentence stating the methodological or biological insight, then one sentence stating the study that operationalizes it.`
   `references/examples/abstract/template-b.md`

3. Version 3 (Multiple Contributions) — systematic review / meta-analysis with several prespecified contributions.
   `Version 3: When the paper has more than one prespecified contribution, describe each contribution together with its quantitative result.`
   `references/examples/abstract/template-c.md`
