# Methods Pre-Writing Questions

`Before writing Methods, answer: (1) what is the study design and reporting standard, and (2) for each subsection (participants, intervention/exposure, outcomes, sample size, randomization/bias control, statistical analysis), what was done, why, and how is bias controlled?`

```text
Before writing, answer:
(1) Study design and matching reporting checklist (CONSORT/STROBE/PRISMA/STARD).
(2) For each subsection:
    - What was done? (procedure, instrument, model)
    - Why was it done that way? (rationale tied to study aim or known bias)
    - How is bias controlled? (blinding, prespecification, validated instrument,
      sensitivity analysis)
```

## Recommended action

1. Organize the answers in a mind map or table before writing prose.
2. Map each subsection to specific items in the reporting checklist; you will paste those page or paragraph numbers into the checklist before submission.
