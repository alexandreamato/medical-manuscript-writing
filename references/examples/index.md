## Usage

1. Pick one template from a section guide.
2. Open the matching examples file.
3. Reuse the sentence logic, not exact wording.
4. Verify every citation in your draft against PubMed/DOI; never invent references.
5. After every revision, re-check that tables, figures, and citations appear in order of first mention.
